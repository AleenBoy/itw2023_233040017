Mike Mentzer Insane Training

ditulis oleh Aqillah Lean. Pada 20 Oktober 2023.

Mike believed that less is more when it comes to training. This made him a strong advocate of a training philosophy known as High-Intensity Training, developed by Arthur Jones, which lifted heavyweights with relatively few repetitions and emphasized quality over quantity. According to Mentzer, this exercise style produced the best results because it minimizes muscle damage and allows for more effective workouts. However, his recommendations were controversial because they challenged the conventional wisdom that more is better when training.

ABOUT MIKE MENTZER’S WORKOUT


Mike Mentzer’s “Heavy Duty” workout routine was unique because it was a high-intensity training program that was only performed one day per week. It consisted of very few exercises per workout, each performed for one set only.

MIKE MENTZER’S WORKOUT ROUTINE:



  • CHEST WORKOUT

    • Incline Dumbbell Flyes: 5 sets at 8 reps each set.

    Bodyweight Dips: 5 sets x 6 reps (60 seconds between sets)

    Incline Barbell Bench Press: 5 sets at 6-8 reps each set.

    Flat barbell bench press: 5sets at 6-8 reps

    Flat dumbbell fly: 5 sets at 6-8 reps

  • BACK WORKOUT

    • Straight arm Pulldown: 5 sets of 5-8 reps

    Close Grip cable pulldown: 5 sets of 8-10 reps

    Barbell Bent-Over Row: 5 sets of 5 reps

    Standing barbell shrug: 5 sets of 8-10 reps

    Barbell Upright row: 5 sets of 6-8 reps


  • SHOULDER WORKOUT

    • Bent over dumbbell raise: 5 sets 8-10 reps

    Seated machine press: 5 sets 6-8 reps

    Dumbbell side lateral raise: 5 sets 8-10 reps

    Front barbell raise: 5 sets 8-10 reps

  • LEG WORKOUT

    • Single leg Extension: 5 sets 8-10 reps

    Single leg hamstring Curl: 5 sets 8-10 reps

    Barbell squats: 5 sets 6-8 reps

    Leg Press: 5 sets 6-8 reps

    Standing Calf Raise: 5 sets 10-15 reps

  • ARM ROUTINE

    • Supinated chin-up: 5 sets 6-8 reps

    Bodyweight dips: 5 sets 10 reps

    Barbell preacher curl: 5 sets 8-10 reps

    Cable triceps pushdowns: 5 sets 8-10 reps

    Standing dumbbell curl: 5 sets 8-10 reps
